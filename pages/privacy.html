MMHN Moms App Privacy Policy

This web application is provided for informational purposes only, and does not collect, store or share personally identifiable information.

Log files contain data on the information that was accessed, originating IP addresses, internet service providers, device types, browser types and operating systems used, none of which contains any personally identifiable information.

As such, this web application is in compliance with all applicable state, national and international laws and standards regarding the protection of personal information.
